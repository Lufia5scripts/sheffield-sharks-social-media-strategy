# Sheffield Sharks Seasonal Ticket Analysis
To conduct this analysis, I compiled season ticket pricing data through direct research across all Super League Basketball clubs. This involved reviewing official ticketing pages, comparing multiple seating tiers, and verifying ambiguous information where necessary. Unlike standard matchday pricing, season ticket structures vary significantly between clubs, with differences in loyalty discounts, premium seating packages, and in one notable case installment based payment options. These variations required careful interpretation to ensure that each club’s offering was compared on fair and consistent terms.

The dataset used in this report focuses exclusively on adult season ticket prices, capturing both standard and premium tiers across the league. The aim is not simply to list prices, but to contextualise where the Sheffield Sharks sit within the broader market. By applying descriptive statistics, such as measures of central tendency and indicators of spread, this analysis highlights how the Sharks position themselves relative to budget clubs, mid‑tier competitors, and high‑end premium teams. It also identifies unique pricing strategies within the league, such as the Rider's monthly installment model, and evaluates how such innovations could inform future pricing decisions for the Sharks.
